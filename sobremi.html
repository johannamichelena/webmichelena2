About me

I´m a Graphic Designer

My name is Johanna Michelena and I am a graphic designer from Buenos Aires, Argentina. During the Graphic Design career at the University of Buenos Aires, I have created different projects, designing for web pages, packaging, brands, editorial, social networks, user experiences (UI/UX), and more.

I like everything related to design, and I am a fan of photography, illustration, fashion and travel, I enjoy every moment and there is always something new to discover and grow.
